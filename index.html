Current Research Interests

I am interested in perception in autonomous systems. I am fascinated with the different sensing mechanisms a machine can use to interact with the physical world. I aim to understand and explore different representations these sensors can create and use machine learning and AI techniques to apply this understanding to real world applications. My most recent work involves Multi Object tracking in videos using a transformer based neural network architecture.
