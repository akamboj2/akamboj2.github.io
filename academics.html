Current Research Interests

My research interest involves perception in Autonomous Vehicles (AV). I'm interested in multi-modal multi-task learning used in AVs (learning how to use multiple sensors to do the many subtasks in driving simultaneously). More specifically, I am currently studying sensor fusion techniques for robust object detection in foggy and rainy weather, using lidar, radar, and camera data.
