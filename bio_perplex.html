Current Research Spotlight

My recent work, C3T (Cross-Modal Transfer Through Time), tackles a fundamental challenge in AI: how to teach models to understand activities by learning from both video and sensor data simultaneously. This approach achieved a 43.5% improvement in accuracy across multiple datasets and shows promise for applications ranging from healthcare monitoring to brain-computer interfaces.

At Google this summer, I pushed the boundaries of temporal reasoning in AI, developing systems that can understand complex videos over time‚Äîbringing us 10% closer to human-level performance on challenging reasoning tasks.
